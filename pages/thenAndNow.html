Then And Now: 2022 Evan vs. 2026 Ev

My jouney through college has always been a little... strange. When I first came to IUI, I was planning on being a photography major. Then, during the summer prior to my Freshman year, I decided to switch to a degree in Visual Communication Design at the Herron School of Art. THEN, only a week before my first semester started, I was told by my honors advisor that a better program for me was Media Arts and Science (MAS).

I wasn't sure exactly what MAS was, but I decided to dive in headfirst anyway. Now, instead of being at the Herron school of art, I was at the Luddy school of informatics. I had no idea at the time, but that switch would drasticaly change my entire trejectory in life.

I Am

I am a kind, hardworking, and open-minded member of IUPUI's Bepko scholars.
